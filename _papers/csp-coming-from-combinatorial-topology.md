title: "CSP coming from combinatorial topology"
date:  2025-07-15 00:00:00 +0100
abstract: "
A graph \\(G\\) is intrinsically \\(k\\)-linked if every spatial embedding of \\(G\\)
contains \\(k\\) pairwise topologically non-trivially linked cycles. Intrinsically \\(k\\)-linked
graphs exist, but the known constructions are very large. At the same time, the
complete graph \\(K_6\\) is already intrinsically \\(2\\)-linked. We show that \\(K_{3k}\\) is not
intrinsically \\(k\\)-linked for any \\(k \\ge 3\\). To do this, we formulate \\(k\\)-linkedness as a
search problem, which will yield embeddings of various small graphs without any
\\(k\\)-link with pairwise odd linking numbers. We will realize such embedding of \\(K_9\\)
geometrically using unlinks, and finally generalize this construction to \\(K_{3k}\\) for any
\\(k \\ge 3\\).
"
